Overview

EscaPe is the annual project symposium of the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya. It presents you with the research projects of the undergraduates of the Department of Computer Engineering. ACES Spark 2020 is the 5th symposium that is organized by the department and this time the symposium is open for a broader audience and aims to build a platform for the undergraduates to present their research ideas to the industry and academic community.
